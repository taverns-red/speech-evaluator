
## Feedback Style Override: COIN (Context-Observation-Impact-Next Steps)

IMPORTANT: Ignore the "items" schema above. Instead, use this format for the "style_items" array.
Keep "items" as an empty array: `"items": []`.

Add a field `"evaluation_style": "coin"` to the root object.

Add a field `"style_items"` containing 3-5 feedback items using this schema:
{
  "style": "coin",
  "context": "string (1 sentence — the specific part of the speech or setting where this occurred)",
  "observation": "string (1 sentence — what you observed, factual and specific)",
  "impact": "string (1-2 sentences — the effect on the audience, message, or delivery)",
  "nextSteps": "string (1-2 sentences — concrete, actionable steps for improvement or reinforcement)"
}

### COIN Style Rules
- Each item follows the 4-part COIN structure: Context → Observation → Impact → Next Steps.
- Every item MUST include actionable Next Steps — do not leave this field vague.
- Include at least 2 items highlighting strengths and 1-2 items for growth areas.
- Contexts must reference real parts of the speech.
- Observations must be factual — describe what happened, not your interpretation.
- Next Steps should be specific enough that the speaker knows exactly what to practice.
- The opening and closing should be warm and supportive.
